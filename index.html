Ignite.

Ignite is a fully anonymous end-to-end encrypted online platform based on the idea of the OTR Messaging Platform. Initialy we started with this idea because we saw the impact the FBI vs Apple legal case had on cryptography as a whole and we saw the desperate need the common person today have for privacy.

No Installation Needed

Built on free online platforms that support every browser.

Based on the safest Encryption methods

Ignite utilises a combination of symmetric and asymmetric cryptography in order to achieve maximum security,it is based on a combination of AES symmetric-key algorithm with 128 bits key length, the Diffieâ€“Hellman key exchange with 1536 bits group size, and the SHA-1 hash function.

File-Sharing

Ignite let's you create a timed peer-to-peer link in order to share your files with friends safely and private. After the file has been downloaded there will be a small time frame in which the user will be able to initialise another install before the link gets deleted for ever from our servers

Finding People

There are 2 ways to find someone to communicate either by scanning their personal QR code or by using their personal generated hash.
