The anonymous connecting platform.

Ignite is a fully anonymous end-to-end encrypted online platform based on the idea of the OTR platform(Off The Record). Initialy we started with this idea because we saw the impact the FBI vs Apple legal case had on the everyday people, we saw the desperate need the common person today have for privacy.

Based on the safest Encryption methods

File-Sharing

Ignite let's you create a timed peer-to-peer link in order to share your files with friends safely and private. After the file has been downloaded there will be a smart time frame in which the user will be able to initialise another install before the link gets deleted for ever from our servers
